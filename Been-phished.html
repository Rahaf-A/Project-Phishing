Been Phished?

What to do if you've been phished

Being a victim is devastating and terrifying. Many people are not sure what to do after they’re sure they were scammed. Here are some steps a victim can follow after being attacked.

Disconnect your device.
This ensures that the malware wouldn’t spread to other devices and prevent it from sending out your sensitive information. If also ensures that no one can remotely access your device.

Backup your data immediately
This is important to do before it’s destroyed by the attacker. Attackers destroy your data to ensure that you lose your way to recovering your information. Make sure you always have your files backed and safe.

Scan your system for malware.
The attack could be quietly occurring without the victim noticing. It’s important to check your system for malware regularly or if you think you might’ve fallen for a phishing attack.

Change your credentials.
Ensure the attacker has no access to your accounts. Applies to all of your online accounts like email, online banking, social media, shopping accounts, and other impotant accounts you may have.
